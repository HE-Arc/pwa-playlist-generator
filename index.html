What is it ?

This application allows you to generate your own website to host and share your music. The generated application is a Progressive Web App (more info here).

To generate your PWA, fill the inputs and then click the GENERATE PWA button.

Title : your application title

Root folder : the root folder containing your audio files

Icon : your application icon

Download the generated zip file.
